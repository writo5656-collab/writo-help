## 1️⃣ Placing an Order

Getting your work written takes less than two minutes to set up.

1. Go to **[writo.shop](https://writo.shop)** and **log in**.
2. Click **"Place Order"** on your dashboard.
3. Fill in the **service type, number of pages, urgency, and project details**.
4. Proceed to payment.
5. Your order is **confirmed immediately**.

> 💡 **Tip:** Works on both laptop and mobile browsers — no app install needed.

## ❓ FAQ

**How do I place an order?**
After logging in, click "Place Order" on your dashboard. Fill in the service type, pages, urgency, and project details, then proceed to payment (COD or online). Your order is confirmed immediately.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
Cash on Delivery (COD) for physical delivery, or online via Razorpay — UPI, credit/debit cards, netbanking, and wallets.

**Can I cancel my order?**
Yes, within 6 hours of placement if the status is "pending payment" or "paid." No cancellations once processing starts. Refunds take 7–10 business days.

**How are extra pages charged?**
Base price covers 300 pages; each extra page is ₹7, calculated automatically as you type.

**Do you offer revisions?**
Yes — one free revision within 7 days of delivery. Extra revisions may carry a small fee.

**Is my data safe?**
Yes — all files and order details are encrypted and never shared with third parties.

**Where's the fastest place to get help?**
[Discord](https://discord.gg/yGznrCPvBN) — active 24/7.
