## 2️⃣ Payment Options

- **Cash on Delivery (COD)** — for physical delivery
- **Online** via **Razorpay** — UPI, credit/debit cards, netbanking, wallets

💡 **Tip:** Price updates automatically as you enter your page count — no need to calculate it yourself.

---

## 3️⃣ How Pricing Works

- Base price includes the **first 300 pages**
- Every page after that costs **₹7/page**
- Total is calculated live on the order form

---

## 4️⃣ Cancellations & Refunds

1. You can cancel **within 6 hours** of placing the order.
2. Cancellation only works while status is **"pending payment"** or **"paid."**
3. Once processing has started, the order **can't be cancelled**.
4. Refunds are processed within **7–10 business days**.

---

## 5️⃣ Revisions

- **One free revision** within **7 days** of delivery if the work doesn't match your original instructions.
- Additional revisions after that may carry a **small fee**.

## 7️⃣ FAQ Highlights

**Q:** How do I place an order?
**A:** After logging in, click "Place Order" on your dashboard. Fill in the service type, pages, urgency, and project details, then proceed to payment (COD or online). Your order is confirmed immediately.

**Q:** What payment methods do you accept?
**A:** Cash on Delivery (COD) for physical delivery, or online via Razorpay — UPI, credit/debit cards, netbanking, and wallets.

**Q:** Can I cancel my order?
**A:** Yes, within 6 hours of placement if status is "pending payment" or "paid." No cancellations once processing starts. Refunds take 7–10 business days.

**Q:** How are extra pages charged?
**A:** Base price covers 300 pages; each extra page is ₹7, calculated automatically as you type.

**Q:** Do you offer revisions?
**A:** Yes — one free revision within 7 days of delivery. Extra revisions may carry a small fee.

**Q:** Is my data safe?
**A:** Yes — all files and order details are encrypted and never shared with third parties.
